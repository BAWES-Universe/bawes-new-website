# Screen Sharing

**Status:** 🟢 LIVE (inherited from WorkAdventure)

Share your screen with people in your conversation bubble. Accessible via the screenShareButton in the in-world UI.

## How It Works

- Click the screen share button in the conversation bubble
- Your screen is shared with everyone in your proximity bubble
- Toggle on/off — works alongside audio/video in the same bubble
- Multiple users can screen share simultaneously
